1. Eligibility

  • You must be between 23 and 55 years of age at the time of application.

  • You must have a clean driving record with no major traffic violations or criminal offences.
  • You must be physically and mentally fit to operate a motor vehicle for extended periods.
  • Preference is given to applicants with prior professional driving experience, though it is not mandatory.
